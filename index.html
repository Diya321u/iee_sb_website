About IEEE

IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is the world’s largest professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity.

Vision

IEEE will be essential to the global technical community and to technical professionals everywhere, and be universally recognized for the contributions of technology and of technical professionals in improving global conditions.

Mission

IEEE’s core purpose is to foster technological innovation and excellence for the benefit of humanity.

Global Presence

IEEE has a strong global presence with members in over 160 countries, supporting professionals and students through societies, conferences, standards, and student branches worldwide.

Benefits of IEEE Membership

  • Access to high-quality technical publications and research
  • Professional networking and global collaboration
  • Participation in conferences, workshops, and technical events
  • Leadership development and career growth opportunities

Events Organised

The IEEE Student Branch at St. Thomas College of Engineering and Technology, Kannur (IEEE SB STM), established in December 2023, is a vibrant student-led community driven by innovation, learning, and impact. Guided by our Branch Counsellor, Dr. Sreekesh Namboodiri T, the branch creates opportunities for students to explore technology beyond classrooms.

IEEE SB STM empowers students through hands-on workshops, technical talks, hackathons, competitions, and collaborative projects that focus on real-world problem solving. We encourage curiosity, creativity, and collaboration while helping members build technical expertise, leadership skills, and a strong professional network.

Rooted in the core values of integrity, inclusiveness, global outlook, and service to humanity, IEEE SB STM strives to nurture future-ready engineers who innovate responsibly and contribute meaningfully to society and the global engineering community.

Societies & Affinity Groups

IEEE offers a wide range of technical societies and affinity groups that enable members to collaborate, specialize, and contribute to technological advancement and professional development across the globe.

IEEE Computer Society (CS)

Focuses on computing technologies including software development, artificial intelligence, data science, cybersecurity, and emerging computing trends.

IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES)

Dedicated to the advancement of power generation, transmission, distribution, renewable energy, and sustainable power systems.

IEEE Communications Society (ComSoc)

Concentrates on communication technologies such as wireless networks, internet technologies, signal processing, and telecommunications.

IEEE Robotics & Automation Society (RAS)

Promotes innovation in robotics, automation systems, control systems, and intelligent machines.

Women in Engineering (WIE)

Supports and encourages the participation of women in engineering, leadership, research, and technical careers.

Young Professionals (YP)

Assists recent graduates and early-career professionals in networking, career growth, and professional skill development.

SIGHT

Focuses on applying technology to humanitarian challenges and improving quality of life in underserved communities.
